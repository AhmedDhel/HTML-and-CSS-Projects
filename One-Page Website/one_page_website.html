Sushi

The sushi theory often refers to the Sushi Train metaphor,
which is a tool used in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
to help individuals observe and unhook themselves from unhelpful thoughts.
This metaphor compares the mind to a sushi train, where thoughts are represented
as sushi plates, and the observer's awareness is akin to the train's conductor.
By learning to notice and observe all thoughts, individuals can better manage
their mental health and align their awareness with their values.

Making sushi is a very guarded tradition.
Every restaurant that does it has their own tried and true methods for preparing the rice,
pairing flavors, and coming up with specialty rolls etc.
I have been studying sushi methods for several years and I still have not come across anything
close to a generalists guide to building rolls and pairing flavors. Does anyone have such a resource
they would be willing to share? Thanks in advance!

BBQ

BBQ theory encompasses various aspects of barbecue, including:
Techniques and Science: Understanding the science behind cooking, such as the effects
of heat on meat and the role of smoke in flavor development.
Philosophical Aspects: Barbecue is viewed as an existential experience,
reflecting themes of freedom and responsibility, and is a medium for self-expression through food choices.
Myths and Truths: There are many misconceptions about BBQ, such as the idea that more smoke is
better or that one type of wood works for all meats. These myths can be debunked to enhance the BBQ experience.
Practical Tips: Effective BBQ involves careful selection of meat, marinades, and cooking
techniques, which can significantly impact the final product.
For a deeper understanding, you can explore articles on BBQ theory and its applications.
